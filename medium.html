Game Menu

Trivia Quiz Game
Concept: Players answer multiple-choice questions across various topics.
  • Timed questions to increase difficulty.
  • Leaderboard for high scores.
  • Categories (history, science, pop culture) with varying levels of difficulty.
Mastermind
Concept: A code-breaking game where one player creates a secret code and the other player guesses it.
  • Multiple rounds with increasing difficulty.
  • Feedback on each guess.
  • Score tracking based on time and accuracy.
Text Adventure Game
Concept: Players navigate through a story by making choices that affect the outcome.
  • Branching paths with multiple endings.
  • Inventory system for items collected.
  • Puzzles and riddles to solve.
Text-Based Role-Playing Game (RPG)
Concept: Players create a character and navigate through a world filled with challenges and quests.
  • Character stats affect gameplay.
  • Random encounters with enemies or NPCs.
  • Choices influence story and progression.
